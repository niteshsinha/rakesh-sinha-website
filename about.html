About

The Lucknow-based photographer who has made an indelible mark in the world of art, culture, and theatre photography over the last four decades. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for capturing the essence of great Indian classical musicians and dancers, this photographer has created a vast and valuable archive of photographs that are truly timeless..

His photographs capture the memorable moods of these artists in their live concerts, providing a glimpse into the soul of their music and dance. The black and white enlargements exhibited by this photographer in various music and dance festivals, important organizations, banners, and government academies have been well-acclaimed by the press and media, establishing them as an active photojournalist..

Through his lens, the photographer has brought to life the magic of Indian classical music and dance, preserving the heritage of this rich tradition for generations to come. Their contribution to the world of art and culture photography has been immense, and their work serves as a testament to the power of visual storytelling.

Awarded Rashtriya Yuva Prathisthan Award for cultural photography. UP Sangeet Natak Academy Award for Cultural Photography (2007) . Sanskar Bharti’s Samman and Bharatyam’s Kala Award.
